RECIPE

"FRIED RICE"

  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce

Methods:

  • 1. Suate onion and garlic in hot pan with oil until the garlic became golden brown.
  • 2. Put the left over Rice and stir well.
  • 3. Season with salt and pepper,also you can add soy sauce and oyster sauce.
  • 4. Put your scramble eggs.
  • 5. Stir well until all ingredients are mixed.
  • 6. Remove from the pan and serve it nice and warm.
